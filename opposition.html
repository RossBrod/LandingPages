Legal Framework

Burden First, Merits Second

The system identifies the applicable burden framework, determines whether the movant has failed at a threshold step, and forces burden-based arguments to appear first when dispositive.

"Many motions fail before the merits. Most lawyers miss that."

Identifies the burden framework for the motion type
Detects threshold failures before reaching the merits
Forces burden arguments first when they are dispositive

Authority Analysis

Distinguish Authority Systematically

Every opponent-cited case is analyzed. Strongest distinctions are ranked. Outdated, narrowed, or non-binding authority is flagged. Judge-facing distinction language is generated.

"Not all distinctions are equal. The system knows which ones matter."
